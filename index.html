Objective // Approach

Data is never neutral. Every dataset contains a narrative structure — a tension, a rupture, a pattern that resists reduction to numbers alone. My work translates raw data into visceral, cognitively immediate experiences that bypass sanitized dashboards and force direct engagement with the underlying reality.

About // System Profile

I'm an analyst and creative strategist in transition toward data-driven roles at the intersection of business analysis, visual design, and strategic storytelling.

My approach rejects the safety of standard dashboards. Each project starts from a question: what does the data feel like when you remove the distance? The technical stack is a means — not the point. The point is cognitive friction that produces insight.

Background in music, creative entrepreneurship, and management studies. Currently building analytical systems (Mosaic, Crescendo) that apply the same visual rigor to business intelligence.

Focus Data storytelling, Visual analytics, Strategic analysis
Stack JavaScript, Three.js, WebGL, GLSL, Python, D3.js
Domain Geopolitics, Climate, Business intelligence
Approach Translate data into unavoidable cognitive experiences
